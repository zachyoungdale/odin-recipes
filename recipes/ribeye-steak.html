Ribeye Steak Recipe

Making a ribeye steak taste this good is super easy! Prep time will take about 5 minutes and total cook time should range from 12-15 minutes

Ingredients

  • One 12oz ribeye steak of choice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • 2tbs of butter

Directions

  1. First, season raw steak with salt and pepper
  2. Preheat pan to medium-high heat and drizzle olive oil in the pan
  3. Place steak in pan and let it cook for 5 minutes
  4. After five minutes, flip the steak over and baste with butter, garlic, rosemary, and thyme. Cook for five more minutes
  5. After those five minutes, flip steak over and season again and cook for 2 more minutes
  6. After that you should have a perfectly medium-rare ribeye steak!
