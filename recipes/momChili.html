Mom's Chili Recipe

This is the famous chili recipe that I was raised on as a kid. My mom would always make a big batch of this and use it for many purposes like chili and cheese enchiladas or just a plain bowl of chili with some crackers. This chili recipe is sure to make you feel super cozy and make you feel like you're back at home!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of ground beef
  • One large onion
  • 5 cans of black label ranch style beans
  • One can of diced tomatoes
  • Two packs of McCormick chili seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Dice up onion and add with ground beef to a pot with olive oil on medium heat with salt and pepper
  2. Cook that for five minutes stirring consistently
  3. turn heat down to low and add the beans, diced tomatoes, and dry seasonings
  4. Let simmer in the pot for 30-45 minutes and serve!
